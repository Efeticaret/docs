Internap AgileFILES¶

Sign up for Internap AgileCLOUD (includes AgileFILES object storage).

Connecting¶

Connection Profile¶

Download the Internap AgileFILES Connection Profile for preconfigured settings.

Manual Configuration¶

Enter the following information in the bookmark:

  • Protocol: OpenStack

  • Server: auth.storage.santa-clara.internapcloud.net

  • Port: 443

  • Username: [account/client login name]

  • Password: [your client password]

For more user information please log in to the Internap Customer Portal.
